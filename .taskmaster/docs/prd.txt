Project: Dr. Jan Duffy Realtor - Homes at Lone Mountain

Overview:
Dr. Jan Duffy is a real estate agent specializing in buying and selling homes in the Lone Mountain area. The goal is to create a digital toolkit and workflow that streamlines property listings, client communications, and local market analysis.

Unique Selling Points:
- Personalized service, deep local knowledge, and a proven track record in Lone Mountain real estate.

Key Features:
- Showcase Lone Mountain properties with high-quality images and detailed descriptions.
- Integrate lead capture forms and automated follow-up.
- Provide neighborhood insights and market trends.
- Enable easy scheduling for showings and consultations.
- Optimize for SEO and local search.
- Ensure mobile-friendly, fast-loading pages.

Content Strategy:
- Weekly blog posts about Lone Mountain market trends, home buying tips, and community events.

Lead Nurturing:
- Automated email sequences for new leads, follow-up reminders, and post-sale check-ins.

Integrations:
- Integrate with Homebot, RealScout, and CloudCMA for property valuations and client engagement.

Branding:
- Brand Colors: #0A2540, #3A8DDE, #F7F9FC, #16B286
- Accent Colors: Buyer: #3A8DDE, Seller: #16B286
- Typography: Modern, clean sans-serif
- Imagery: High-resolution photos of Lone Mountain, local landmarks, and happy clients

Target Audience:
- Home buyers and sellers interested in Lone Mountain.
- Relocation clients seeking local expertise.

Success Metrics:
- Increased qualified leads.
- Higher engagement on property pages.
- Improved client satisfaction and conversion rates.

Automation & Recurring Workflows:
- Weekly blog publishing
- Monthly market report generation
- Quarterly website SEO audits
- Regular CRM data cleanup

Tech Stack:
- Next.js, Tailwind CSS, shadcn/ui
- Follow design system and coding standards
