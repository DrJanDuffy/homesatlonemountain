Lone Mountain has consistently shown strong appreciation. With median home prices around $550,000 and year-over-year growth of 4.5%, it's both a great place to live and a solid investment.

## Family-Friendly Community

Parks, hiking trails, and community spaces make Lone Mountain perfect for families. The neighborhood has a true sense of community that's hard to find in larger cities.

## Easy Access to Everything

Located in Northwest Las Vegas, Lone Mountain offers:
- Quick access to Summerlin and Downtown Summerlin shopping
- Easy freeway access via the 215 Beltway
- 20 minutes to the Las Vegas Strip
- Close to Red Rock Canyon for outdoor recreation
